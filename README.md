## Bank-Loan-Approval-Analysis - Tableau Dashboard
### Tableau
### Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing loan approval data to understand customer behavior, approval trends, and credit risk indicators. An interactive Tableau dashboard was developed to support decision-making for credit and risk teams.
The dashboard is designed from a banking and underwriting perspective, simulating real business questions faced during loan approval processes.

### Business Objective

Analyze loan approval and rejection patterns

Identify key factors influencing loan approval

Highlight high-risk yet high-value customer segments

Enable interactive exploration for management and credit teams

### Dashboard Highlights

Loan Approval Summary: Approved vs Not Approved loan distribution

Income Analysis: Applicant income distribution and its impact on approvals

Marital Status Analysis: Married vs Not Married approval trends

Dependents Impact: Approval patterns by number of dependents

Risk Segmentation: High-risk and high-value customer identification

Loan Term Analysis: Approval rate across short, medium, and long-term loans

Property Area Analysis: Approval comparison across Urban, Semiurban, and Rural areas
